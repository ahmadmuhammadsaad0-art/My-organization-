About YCC

We are a youth-led organization focused on community action to address climate change. Our work centers on education, hands-on projects, and partnerships that deliver measurable benefits: cleaner air, resilient local food systems, and stronger community livelihoods.

Our mission: Mobilize and support young people to lead local climate action that protects both people and nature.

How to take action

Community Tree Planting

Organize planting days with schools and neighbourhood groups. Trees reduce heat and improve stormwater absorption.

  • Plan: map safe public spaces
  • Act: secure seedlings and volunteers
  • Maintain: watering & protection plan
